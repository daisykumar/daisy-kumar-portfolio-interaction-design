A Little About Me

Greetings!

I'm Daisy, based in Oslo, Norway. I combine research, personalised approaches, and iterative processes- to conceptualise and transform complex problems into user-friendly solutions.


My experience of 2 years with university projects as an Interaction Design student at Høyskolen Kristiania (fagskole) and industry experience, taught me how to balance business goals and engineering constraints while unrelentingly advocating for the users.


Being Glocal: Driven by discovering the intricacies of different people and cultures, I often network, facilitate Design Sprint workshops or join international events like America’s ‘Global Design Sprints’ (remote) or Oslo’s Women In Tech, IxDA events, and the like.


When I’m not working: I go trekking, camping, spending time with friends and family and enjoying tasty food! :)
